Benedict Wild

I am a PhD Student in the Cognitive Neuroscience Lab at the German Primate Center. I am interested in how sensory information, particularly visual motion, is encoded in the brain.

I am also a huge fan of distance running, both as a runner myself and as a fan.

In my blog I am planning to write about issues in both, science and running. I might split it up in two separate blogs at one point in the future.

I'm currently using this space mostly as a playground to learn some HTML and CSS. Expect frequent changes.
